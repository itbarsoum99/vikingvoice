Rat Race

by Audrey Briggs creative

Neighborhoods of cookie cutter houses

flowers scarcer by the day

Financial worries replace imagination

Childish spark put into a responsibility-defined cage


Our life is put into place before we can decide-

Already successful enough

but never satisfied

Do you win life if you’re rich

or keep going until you die?


“Do what you love”

until what you love 

doesn’t make 

enough money

“Forge your own path

but stay on top 

of your studies.”


Everyone says 

they want

more

but more becomes

less 

defined 

by the day 


Intuitive minds

eventually 

corrupted 

by the 

never-ending 

rat race.

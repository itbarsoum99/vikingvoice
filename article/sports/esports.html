North Mecklenburg Participates in Esports

by Khai Mathlage sports

[This article current as of December 10, 2023]

One passion for many students is video games. Many go home and play for hours online with their friends working towards improving their teamwork and building friendships. With the rise of video games, for years, students have pushed for video games to be included as a school-based extracurricular activity. Over the last twenty years, Esports have become more common, rapidly growing to include in-person and online tournaments around the world, eventually reaching our high schools and universities. This is the second year that the Varsity Esports and STEM League (VESL) and the University of North Carolina at Greensboro have partnered with Charlotte Mecklenburg Schools to create Esports tournaments. 

North Mecklenburg High School has become a major team within the league, with its Valorant A-team striving towards regionals. How can you join the team? Stop by the Esports club on Thursdays between 2:30 to 3:30 in room J201 to inquire about joining the team and being added to the VESL discord server. While there, you will earn a sense of comradery, connect with others who share your passions, and enjoy playing your favorite game. 

Currently, North Mecklenburg has teams for Valorant, Super Smash Bros Ultimate, Overwatch 2, Fortnite, Call of Duty, and more.

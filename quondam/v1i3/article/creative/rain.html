Rain

by Audrey Briggs creative

So I lean my head back and laugh 

Let the rain fall across my face,

remind me that it’s okay 

to set my own pace


Movies tell me to go to parties

get drunk and stay popular at all costs

My parents want me to study 

“Prepare for college, or you’ll be completely lost”


But I’m more focused on today

My hair in a messy bun 

The rush to get ready

so that I’m not late


For now,


everything else can wait.


I choose to welcome this moment in 

let it take its coat off and stay 

I know that soon, 

this all will change


And the rain will never feel 

exactly the same 

as how it does today.
